Privacy Policy for StreamScout

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Welcome to StreamScout. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

When you use StreamScout, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication (including Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In).
  • Usage Data: We collect data about your interactions with the App, such as your watch history, watchlists, ratings, and preferences. This data is stored securely using Google Cloud Firestore.
  • Analytics and Crash Data: We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous diagnostic and usage data to improve the App's performance and stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the personalized features of the App.
  • To synchronize your watch history and preferences across your devices securely.
  • To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs to improve the overall user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) servers. We implement industry-standard security measures, including Firebase App Check, to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. Our application utilizes a secure Application Programming Interface (API) proxy to protect third-party API keys (such as TMDB and OMDb) and ensure zero secrets are stored directly on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App utilizes the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services & Firebase (Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Firestore)
  • Apple (Sign-In with Apple)
  • TMDB and OMDb: Used for fetching movie and TV show metadata. No personal user data is transmitted to these services.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data. You can delete your account directly from the App's settings or by contacting us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@streamscout.app.
